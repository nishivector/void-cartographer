## How to Play

1. **Movement:** WASD keys to move around
2. **Look:** Mouse to look around (click to lock pointer)
3. **Anchor:** Left-click on glowing geometric shapes to anchor them and map the area
4. **Collect:** Gather cyan crystals to restore resonance charges
5. **Avoid:** Stay away from orange void rifts ‚Äî they'll consume you!
6. **Win:** Map 5 points in each edge world to advance. Complete 5 worlds to win!
